Subpoena Responses

Receiving a government subpoena can be disruptive, stressful, and legally complex. Henley Law provides experienced, strategic counsel in responding to federal and state subpoenas, civil investigative demands, and regulatory inquiries — protecting your rights from day one.

Kya provides legal representation in response to subpoenas and inquiries issued by a wide range of agencies, including the DOJ, FBI, HHS-OIG, DEA, DOL, Congressional Committees, OSHA, SBA-OIG, DoD-OIG, State Attorneys General, and State Licensing Boards.
